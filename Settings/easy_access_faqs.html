Plans

How can I see plan information?

Plans are categorized into individual plans and health plans. You can select any plan from drop down option to view detail information.

Premium Calculator

How can I calculate premium?

You need to select plan from premium calculator section and provide various inputs such as age,policy term, sum assured etc. to calculate premium.

My Policy

How can I use My Policy service?

You need to register on My Policy by entering Policy Number and DOB. You will then recieve OTP to your registered mobile number. After Validating OTP, You need to set 4 digit pin. Registered user can use the service by providing correct username and password.

I am already using My Policy on my desktop/laptop. Do I need separate registration/user request for mobile application?

Yes. You have to register separately for EASY ACCESS.

How can I view policy Details?

You can click on any policy displayed under 'View Policy' section to view detail policy information.

How can I download various forms?

Various forms are available under 'Request Form' section. From will be emailed as attachment to specified email address.

How can I send the queries?

You can send queries/feedback by selecting categories, sub categories and policy number under 'Queries' section.

How can I logout from My Policy?

You can logout from application by clicking on menu button on right top corner of the application and by selecting logout option.

Contact Us

How can I send queries or feedback to SBI Life Insurance?

You can click on contact numbers provided under 'Contact Us' section to call SBI Lide helpline numbers. You can also send queries/feedback via email displyed under 'Email Us' section.

How can contact SBI Life Insurance to send their representative?

You can enter your personal details and contact details in 'Meet Our advisor' section and click on submit to send request for SBI Life Insurance's repesentative.
