^Guaranteed Additions:

Guaranteed Additions are added at a simple rate on the sum of the premiums& paid, at the end of each policy year. The percentage of Guaranteed Addition will be based on the annual# premium selected.

# Annual Premium is the premium payable in a policy year excluding applicable taxes and loading for modal premium

The Guaranteed Additions are defined as percentage of the sum of basic premiums paid& as on the end of any policy year. It will be 5.5%* for Annual premiums of Rs. 15,000 to Rs. 29,000 and 6.0%* for Annual premiums of Rs. 30,000 to Rs. 75,000

* The Guaranteed Additions defined above is at a simple rate and not at compounding rates.
