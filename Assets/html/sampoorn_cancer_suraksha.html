• Introduction

    • One may never consider oneself at risk but with the rising incidence of cancer, one cannot be too sure. From being considered as a terminal disease, the advances in medical science has made cure & recovery possible especially on early detection.

    Treatment of cancer is getting better, increasing life expectancy and offering hope to the afflicted. But this comes at a huge cost and for most, it may mean liquidating the savings of a life time.

    While medical insurance policy may pay for the cost of hospitalization & surgical procedures, there are many other costs which are not covered. E.g. Out of pocket expenses, Travel & Stay in a different city for treatment, cost of care (nurse/additional help), etc. besides loss of employment/earnings.

    Through SBI Life - Sampoorn Cancer Suraksha, an individual, non-participating, non-linked health insurance product, we want to ensure that you do not have to fight cancer alone.

    So, go ahead and secure your family's peace of mind.

  • How does the Benefit structure work

    This product is a simple yet comprehensive cancer care product. You can purchase this product on answering a basic medical questionnaire. You have 3 benefit structures to choose from and the benefits offered under each benefit structure is as below:<

    • Standard - Covers Minor & Major stage cancer
    • Classic - Covers Minor & Major stage cancer, and additional payout on Advanced stage cancer
    • Enhanced - Covers Minor & Major stage cancer with additional payout on Advanced stage cancer, and with benefit of Sum assured reset
      • The payouts for this product are designed in way to provide continuous financial assistance.
